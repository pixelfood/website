The Needs

Strung Jewelry began as a fun project with a friend of mine who had some time after her stint at Arnold Communication. She had started make jewelry as a creative outlet and wanted a way to sell. I came up with the name, logo, color scheme and a flash-based website in the first go-round . This website is a responsive update to that site that I developed using Boostrap, Masonry and a few other addons.
